# Privacy Policy for Caissa

**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

## Introduction
Caissa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign up, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via **Firebase Authentication** to secure your account and sync your data across devices.

### 2. Gameplay and Usage Data
We collect data related to your chess games, including:
- Game moves (PGN)
- Analysis results
- Journal entries and notes
This data is stored securely in **Google Firestore** and locally on your device to provide the App's core functionality.

### 3. Generative AI Features
We use Google Gemini and other AI models to provide features such as:
- **Game Analysis:** Analyzing your chess games to identify mistakes and key moments.
- **AI Coach:** Generating personalized training advice and explanations.
- **Interactive Chat:** Answering your chess-related questions.

When you use these features, the relevant game data (PGN, moves) and your queries are processed by our AI providers. This data is used solely to generate the response and is not used to train public AI models.

### 4. Purchase Information
We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat collects purchase history and subscription status linked to an anonymous App User ID. We do not process or store your credit card or payment information directly; all transactions are handled by Apple's App Store.

### 5. Technical Data
We may collect technical information such as your device model, OS version, and crash logs to help us improve the App's stability and performance.

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
- Sync your chess progress across devices.
- Process and manage your subscriptions.
- Respond to your support requests.
- Generate personalized chess insights using AI.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers essential for the App's operation:
- **Google Firebase:** For authentication and database hosting.
- **Google Gemini (Vertex AI):** For generative AI analysis and coaching.
- **RevenueCat:** For subscription management.

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request the correction of inaccurate data.
- Request the deletion of your account and all associated data. You can delete your account at any time from the App's settings menu.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
